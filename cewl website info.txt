1) to get basic info ::::
      :::in terminal:::
                man cewl               //gives info about cewl

2) to fetch all the words present in a website::::
    :::in terminal:::
                cewl www.website_name.com         //all words will be shown here

3) similar words from a website::::
     :::in terminal::: 
                 cewl -c www.website_name.com
 
4) to fetch emails present in a website::::
     :::in terminal:::
                 cewl -e --email-file /root/Desktop/mail.txt www.website_name.com

                            // space added using \           // or "abc "
